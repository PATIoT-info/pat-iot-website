# QR Code Image

Place your QR code image here with the filename: qr-code.png

## Requirements:

- **Filename**: qr-code.png
- **Format**: PNG (recommended for QR codes)
- **Size**: 200x200px or larger (square)
- **File Size**: Optimized for web
- **Quality**: High quality, clear and scannable

## Location:

The QR code will appear in the Contact section, making it easy for customers to scan and get more information about your company.
