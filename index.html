About Us

PAT IoT Solutions Pvt. Ltd. is a leading home automation company dedicated to transforming ordinary homes into intelligent living spaces. We specialize in manufacturing premium smart touch switch boards, DALI touch dimming systems, and PWM modules, while also providing comprehensive smart home automation solutions.

Based in Hyderabad, Telangana, our team of certified professionals works closely with clients to design and implement customized automation systems that perfectly match their lifestyle and budget. We combine our own manufactured products with carefully selected third-party solutions to deliver complete home automation experiences.

Our commitment to quality, innovation, and customer satisfaction has made us a trusted name in the Indian smart home automation industry.
